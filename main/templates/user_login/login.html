GRMW Accounts

Anyone can create a GRMW account. By creating an account you will be agreeing to GRMW's terms & conditions and privacy policy. Why is creating a GRMW account necessary? GRMW.org uses accounts to track stepwise submissions, which helps provide a personal approach to the stepwise experience. Without an account it would be difficult to separate the information of one submitter from another while hosting all information related to the submission on our site. Creating an account also helps to protect any personal information that might link you to your stepwise submission. For more information about the stepwise process or questions/concerns related to creating an account, please contact the site administrator at infotech@grmw.org.
